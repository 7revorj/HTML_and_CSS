This is a paragraph of brown font. Brown, like the wood the homesteaders use to ply their trade. Brown, like the beavers coat, when the sun is at dusk wane. Brown, like the mired hands of so many sullen woes. Brown, like the brown of a well traveled road.

I see a red door and I want to paint it black. No colors anymore I want them to turn black. I see the girls walk by dressed in their summer clothes. I have to turn my head until my darkness goes.
